About

Carraig Rua

Enjoy a Wonderful Experience

Welcome to Carraig Rua Kilkenny City and our luxurious bed and breakfast home in the Medieval Capital of Ireland.

Our family run bed and breakfast is 3 minutes walk from bus, rail station and a few minutes walk to Langton's Hotel, River Court Hotel, and all hotels in the city, We are within walking distance of pubs, restaurants and historical sites.

Our Hearty full Irish breakfast will keep you going all day. All our guests are delighted to find accommodation with a beautiful garden, tastefully decorated bedroom, Victorian sitting room and our conservatory dining area looking out onto the garden.

Carraig Rua is one of the nearest B and B homes to Lyrath Estate Hotel. Both situated on the Dublin Road N10.

We have private car parking in the heart of the city. Great Location !!!. Our home is ideally placed to have as a base in Kilkenny city to hear traditional Irish music, tasty food from a variety of restaurants, bars and hotels.

Kilkenny Castle, St. Canices Cathedral, Round Tower and Black Abbey are just some of the attractions to see in the city. We are within walking distance of them all.
