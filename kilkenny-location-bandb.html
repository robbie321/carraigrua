Directions

Address:

Carraig Rua, Dublin Road, Kilkenny, R95 EPD1, Ireland.
Getting here from Dublin

From Dublin on the M9: At the roundabout take the first left exit heading for the Old Dublin roundabout. At that roundabout take the third exit heading for city centre. The house is at the bottom of the hill on the right.

Getting here from Cork/Clonmel

From Cork/Clonmel: At the roundabout take the second exit heading on the N10. Go through four roundabouts when you get to the Old Dublin Road roundabout take a left heading for city centre. The house is on the right side at the bottom of hill.

Getting here from Cashel, Urlingford, Freshford and Threecastle

At the roundabout continue straight onto Freshford Road R693. Turn left onto Green St continue to follow R693. At the roundabout take 3 exit onto Castlecomer Road then straight through traffic lights next roundabout take exit 2 then left on to Dublin Road at lights Bed and breakfast on the left hand side Carraig rua.

About Us

Carraig Rua is a family run B and B home. Just a few minute walk to Kilkenny City centre. Private car parking available for all residents. Ideally located to explore the Medieval City and south east area. You are assured a warm and friendly welcome.
